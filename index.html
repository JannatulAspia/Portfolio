About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate from Sylhet Engineering College, affiliated with Shahjalal University of Science & Technology, Bangladesh, with a strong passion for deep learning, computer vision, and AI-driven solutions for real-world challenges. My research focuses on developing interpretable and robust models for medical imaging, pavement crack detection, and multimodal data analysis.

I have hands-on experience with CNNs, Transformers, YOLO, and ensemble architectures, and I specialize in multimodal fusion, explainable AI, weakly-supervised learning, and dataset curation. I am currently seeking Ph.D. opportunities to advance AI research in medical imaging, autonomous detection systems, and interpretable deep learning, while contributing to impactful, real-world applications.

When I'm not coding, you can find me participating in competitive programming contests or exploring new AI research paper.
